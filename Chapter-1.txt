Chapter 1 – The Silent Betrayal

The rain poured heavily on the silent streets of Vardaan City, washing away the blood that had been spilled just moments ago. In the shadows, a figure moved silently — Dushyant, once a loyal soldier, now a betrayed soul.

His family, his name, his life — all taken in a single night. But tonight, vengeance begins.

And from this storm, Shadow Yodha is born.
